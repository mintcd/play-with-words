Emotional Weight
Using 'abandon' can evoke strong feelings as it implies neglect or betrayal. Children felt abandoned when their parents didn't show up for the play.

Not Temporary
'Abandon' often suggests a permanent decision, not just leaving temporarily. After years of struggle, he finally abandoned his dream of becoming an actor.
